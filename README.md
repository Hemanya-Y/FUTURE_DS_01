Task 1: Business Sales Dashboard Project Overview
This project involves building an interactive sales dashboard using Power BI to analyze e-commerce data.
The goal was to identify key performance indicators (KPIs), sales trends, and high-revenue categories to support business decision-making.
Key Features
Interactive Slicer: A global filter was added to allow users to navigate data by Order Date.
KPI Metrics: Displays total business performance, including Total Sales ($2.30$M), Total Orders ($5$K), and Total Quantity ($38$K).
Sales Trends: A line chart visualizing monthly fluctuations to identify seasonal revenue peaks, specifically in December.
Product Performance: A detailed bar chart highlighting the best-selling products, such as the Canon ImageCLASS.
Category Breakdown: Analysis of revenue distribution across Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies.
Business InsightsTop Category: Technology is the primary revenue driver for the business.
Seasonal Peak: Sales reach their highest levels in December, suggesting holiday-driven demand.
Inventory Focus: High-performing products like the Canon ImageCLASS should be prioritized for stock management.
